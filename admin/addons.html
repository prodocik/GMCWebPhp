    Help

    Add-ons & Fees can be added as optional or required line items on reservations. You can apply them to specific rental types or all rentals.

    What do the options mean

    Required: Required add-ons are automatically applied to new reservations. You have the ability to remove them on a case-by-case basis.

    Charged per day: The cost of these add-ons will be multiplied over the number of days in the reservation.

    Charged once: The cost of these add-ons will be added once, not multipled by the number of days.

    Hidden: These add-ons are not visible to renters when requesting a reservation. They are visible to you and can be added to rentals on a case-by-case basis.
