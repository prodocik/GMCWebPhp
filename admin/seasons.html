    Help

    Seasons and events allow you to set adjusted pricing based on the time of year. They are created by adjusting your base pricing by a custom percentage. You can adjust pricing per-vehicle as well if you'd like. Seasons can be one-time only or repeat yearly.

    What do the options mean

    Yearly: Yearly seasons will repeat each year

    Price Change: Prices are adjusted as a percentage of your base pricing. For example, if an RV is $100.00/day, and you set your season pricing to 80%, the season price will be $80/day.

    Minimum Rental: In addition to prices, you can also set season-specific minimum rental durations

    Price Overrides: You can override the season pricing for any vehicle directly
